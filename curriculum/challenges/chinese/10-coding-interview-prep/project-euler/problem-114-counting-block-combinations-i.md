# --description--

A row measuring seven units in length has red blocks with a minimum length of three units placed on it, such that any two red blocks (which are allowed to be different lengths) are separated by at least one black square. 有 17 种方法可以做到这一点。

<img class="img-responsive center-block" alt="Possible ways of placing block with a minimum length of three units, on a row with length of seven units" src="https://cdn.freecodecamp.org/curriculum/project-euler/counting-block-combinations-i.png" style="background-color: white; padding: 10px;" />

有多少种方法可以填充长度为 50 个单位的行？

**Note:** Although the example above does not lend itself to the possibility, in general it is permitted to mix block sizes. 例如，在长度为 8 个单位的行上，你可以使用红色 (3)、黑色 (1) 和红色 (4)。
