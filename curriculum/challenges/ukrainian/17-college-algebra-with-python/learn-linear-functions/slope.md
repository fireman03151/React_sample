## --answers--

`slope = (x1 - x2) / (y1 - y2)`

---

`slope = (x2 - x1) / (y2 - y1)`

---

`slope = (x1 - y1) / (x2 - y2)`

---

`slope = (y2 - y1) / (x2 - x1)`
