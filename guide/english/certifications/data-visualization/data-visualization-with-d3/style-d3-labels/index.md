## Style D3 Labels

### Hint 1

Chain two ` style() ` method together.

### Hint 2

Use ` font-family ` in the first method and ` fill ` in the second method.

### Solution

Add these lines to the end of the chain:

```javascript
  .style("font-size","25px")
  .style("fill","red");
```
